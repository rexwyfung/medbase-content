# Benign Breast Disease (良性乳房疾病)

## 1. Clinical Definition & Local Epidemiology
### Definition of BBD
* **Benign Breast Disease (良性乳房疾病)**: Non-malignant lesions of the **Breast (乳房)**
* Classified clinically by the **ANDI (Aberrations of Normal Development and Involution, 正常發育與退化之異常)** framework
* Represents physiological spectrum variations rather than true pathological disease

### Hong Kong Epidemiology
* Extremely common in HK ♀
* Peak age of presentation: 20-50 yrs
* HA Data: Most common cause of referrals to HA **Breast Clinic (乳房外科門診)**
* **Fibroadenoma (纖維腺瘤)** peak incidence: 20-30 yrs
* **Fibrocystic Change (纖維囊腫變化)** peak incidence: 30-50 yrs

## 2. Surgical Anatomy & Pathophysiology
### Surgical Anatomy of the Breast
* Modified sweat gland located anterior to the **Pectoralis Major (胸大肌)**
* Arterial supply: **Internal Mammary Artery (內乳動脈)** + **Lateral Thoracic Artery (外胸動脈)**
* Lymphatic drainage: 75% drains to the **Axillary Lymph Nodes (腋下淋巴結)**
* **Cooper's Suspensory Ligaments (庫珀氏懸韌帶)**: Fibrous bands providing structural support to the parenchyma

### Pathophysiology by ANDI Classification
| Period | Age | Normal Process | Aberration (BBD) | True Disease |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Early Reproductive | 15-25 | Lobular development | **Fibroadenoma (纖維腺瘤)** | Giant fibroadenoma |
| Mature Reproductive | 25-40 | Cyclic changes | **Cyclical Mastalgia (週期性乳房疼痛)** | Severe incapacitating mastalgia |
| Involution | 35-55 | Lobular/ductal involution | **Breast Cysts (乳房囊腫)**, **Duct Ectasia (乳管擴張)** | Periductal mastitis, Abscess |

## 3. Key Symptoms & Signs
### Breast Lump (乳房腫塊) Characteristics
* **Fibroadenoma (纖維腺瘤)**: Firm, highly mobile ("breast mouse"), non-tender, well-circumscribed
* **Breast Cyst (乳房囊腫)**: Smooth, fluctuant, often tender, rapid onset
* **Lipoma (脂肪瘤)**: Soft, lobulated, subcutaneous

### Nipple Discharge (乳頭分泌物) Patterns
* **Intraductal Papilloma (管內乳突瘤)**: Blood-stained / serosanguinous, typically single duct
* **Duct Ectasia (乳管擴張)**: Multicoloured (green/yellow/white), multi-duct, slit-like nipple retraction
* **Galactorrhoea (溢乳)**: Bilateral, milky (requires exclusion of ↑ prolactin)

### Mastalgia (乳房疼痛) Patterns
* **Cyclical Mastalgia**: Bilateral, dull ache, classically in the upper outer quadrant (UOQ), premenstrual exacerbation
* **Non-cyclical Mastalgia**: Unilateral, sharp/burning, localized (e.g., chest wall pain, costochondritis, trauma)

## 4. Differential Diagnosis & Discriminatory Features
### Differentials for Breast Mass
| Diagnosis | Age | Clinical Features | Key Discriminatory Factor |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fibroadenoma (纖維腺瘤)** | <30 | Firm, highly mobile | USG: well-defined solid oval mass |
| **Fibrocystic Change (纖維囊腫變化)** | 30-50 | Bilateral, lumpy, tender | Fluctuates strictly with menstrual cycle |
| **Breast Cyst (乳房囊腫)** | 35-50 | Tense, round, fluctuant | USG: anechoic, posterior acoustic enhancement |
| **Breast Cancer (乳癌)** | >40 | Hard, irregular, fixed | Skin dimpling, nipple inversion, +ve axillary nodes |
| **Phyllodes Tumour (葉狀腫瘤)** | 40-50 | Rapidly growing mass | Core biopsy required (borderline/malignant potential) |

## 5. Tiered Investigations & Pre-operative Workup (Labs, Imaging, Staging)
### Triple Assessment (三管齊下評估) Protocol
* Mandatory HA protocol for ALL new breast lumps to exclude malignancy
* Step 1: **Clinical Examination (臨床檢查)**
* Step 2: **Imaging (影像檢查)**
* Step 3: **Pathology (病理檢查)**

### Imaging Modalities (影像檢查)
* Age <35: **Ultrasound (超聲波)** is 1st line (dense breasts ↓ mammogram sensitivity)
* Age ≥35: **Bilateral Mammogram (乳房X光造影)** + **Ultrasound (超聲波)**
* **BIRADS Scoring (乳房影像報告和數據系統)**:
  * 1: -ve
  * 2: Benign
  * 3: Probably benign (requires short interval F/U)
  * 4: Suspicious (mandates biopsy)
  * 5: Highly suggestive of malignancy

### Pathological Sampling
* **Fine Needle Aspiration (FNA, 幼針抽吸)**: Diagnostic + therapeutic for cysts. Very limited value for solid lumps.
* **Core Needle Biopsy (CNB, 粗針活檢)**: Gold standard for solid lumps (evaluates tissue architecture & receptor status).

## 6. Management Algorithm & Surgical Indications (Conservative, Medical, HA Protocols)
### HA Protocol for Fibroadenoma (纖維腺瘤)
* Size <2cm & asymptomatic: Reassurance + observe
* **Surgical Indications (Excision Biopsy)**:
  * Size >2cm or rapid ↑ in size
  * Severe patient anxiety / explicit request
  * Diagnostic uncertainty (to definitively exclude **Phyllodes Tumour (葉狀腫瘤)**)
  * Age >40 presenting with a new solid lump

### HA Protocol for Breast Cysts (乳房囊腫)
* Asymptomatic: Reassurance
* Symptomatic/Tense: **FNA (幼針抽吸)**
* Send fluid for cytology ONLY if:
  * Blood-stained
  * Lump persists immediately after aspiration
  * Complex cyst appearance on USG
* Surgical excision indicated if cyst frequently recurs after repeated FNA

### Medical Management of Mastalgia (乳房疼痛)
* 1st Line: Reassurance, firm support bra, PO **NSAIDs / Paracetamol (撲熱息痛)**
* 2nd Line: **Evening Primrose Oil (月見草油)** (mild symptom relief, common in HA)
* 3rd Line (Specialist only): **Danazol (達那唑)** or **Tamoxifen (他莫昔芬)** (for severe cases, limited by heavy side effect profile)

## 7. Key Operative Principles & Consent Issues
### Excision of Fibroadenoma (纖維腺瘤切除術)
* **Anaesthesia**: GA or LA + sedation
* **Incision**: Circumareolar or Langer's lines/skin crease (optimises post-op cosmesis)
* **Operative Steps**:
  * Enucleation of mass along the capsular plane
  * Meticulous haemostasis → ↓ haematoma risk
  * Closure with subcuticular absorbable sutures (e.g., Monocryl)

### Microdochectomy (微小乳管切除術)
* Indication: Single-duct pathological discharge
* Pre-op: Identify discharging duct, cannulate with lacrimal probe
* **Operative Steps**: Exise the targeted duct + immediately surrounding tissue
* Specific consent: Loss of nipple sensation, potential interference with future breastfeeding

### Surgical Consent Issues (手術同意書)
* Bleeding / **Haematoma (血腫)**
* **Wound Infection (傷口感染)**
* Scarring / **Keloid (蟹足腫)**
* Breast asymmetry / Cosmesis changes (乳房外觀改變)
* Recurrence of mass (復發)

## 8. Post-operative Care (ERAS) & Rehabilitation
### Immediate Post-operative Care
* Day-case surgery routine in HA
* Apply firm pressure dressing (壓力敷料) x 24-48 hrs → ↓ dead space / bleeding
* Provide adequate PO analgesia
* Monitor for excessive wound bleeding prior to discharge

### Rehabilitation & Outpatient Follow-up
* Wound review at **SOPD (外科門診)** in 1-2 weeks
* Avoid heavy upper limb lifting x 2 weeks
* Routine shower allowed post-dressing removal
* Discuss final histopathology report at SOPD

## 9. Surgical Complications (Early, Intermediate, Late) & Prognosis
### Early Complications (<30 days)
* **Haematoma (血腫)**: Swelling + bruising. Small → conservative; Large/expanding → return to theatre for evacuation.
* **Surgical Site Infection (傷口感染)**: Erythema + purulent discharge. Rx: PO **Amoxicillin/Clavulanate (安力復)**.

### Late Complications (>30 days)
* **Hypertrophic Scar / Keloid (蟹足腫)**: Common on anterior chest wall. Rx: Silicone gel / steroid injection.
* **Fat Necrosis (脂肪壞死)**: Firm lump forming post-op. May mimic malignancy clinically & radiologically (via calcification).
* **Nipple Retraction (乳頭凹陷)**: Specifically if central ducts are excised.

### Prognosis
* Excellent (100% survival)
* Simple BBD: No ↑ breast cancer risk
* Complex BBD / Atypical Ductal Hyperplasia (ADH): ↑ Risk (requires MDT surveillance)

## 10. Examiner's Pearls (High-yield viva questions and common pitfalls)
* Viva Question: What is the defining clinical sign of a fibroadenoma? Answer: A highly mobile, well-circumscribed, firm mass, classically described as a "breast mouse".
* Viva Question: What is the age cutoff for using Ultrasound versus Mammogram as first-line imaging in Hong Kong? Answer: 35 years old, because increased breast density in younger women significantly reduces mammogram sensitivity.
* Trap: Assuming green or yellow nipple discharge indicates an active bacterial infection. Pearl: Multicoloured, pasty discharge is pathognomonic for benign Duct Ectasia and does not require antibiotics unless true mastitis signs are present.
* Trap: Sending all aspirated cyst fluid for cytological examination. Pearl: Only send cyst fluid if it is blood-stained, if the lump does not completely resolve after aspiration, or if ultrasound shows a complex cyst.
* Pitfall: Discharging a patient with a new breast lump without completing Triple Assessment. You must perform Clinical Examination, Imaging, and Pathology on every new palpable mass to definitively exclude malignancy.
* Pitfall: Confusing a rapidly growing large fibroadenoma with a simple benign lesion. Always suspect a Phyllodes Tumour if a fibroadenoma grows rapidly or exceeds 5cm; core biopsy is mandatory to rule out malignant potential.
