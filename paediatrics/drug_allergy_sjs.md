# Drug Allergy, SJS & DRESS (藥物過敏、史提芬強生症候群及 DRESS)

## 1. Clinical Definition & Local Epidemiology

### SJS/TEN Clinical Definitions (史提芬強生症候群及中毒性表皮壞死症定義)
* **Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS)** (史提芬強生症候群): Life-threatening mucocutaneous hypersensitivity reaction
* **Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN)** (中毒性表皮壞死症): Most severe spectrum of disease → massive epidermal detachment
* Body Surface Area (BSA) detachment classification:
    * **SJS**: <10% BSA
    * **SJS/TEN Overlap**: 10-30% BSA
    * **TEN**: >30% BSA

### DRESS Clinical Definition (藥物疹合併嗜酸血症及全身症狀定義)
* **DRESS**: **D**rug **R**eaction with **E**osinophilia and **S**ystemic **S**ymptoms
* Severe idiosyncratic multi-organ drug reaction
* Target organs:
    * Skin
    * Liver
    * Kidneys
    * Lungs

### Hong Kong Local Epidemiology and Common Triggers (香港本地流行病學及常見誘發藥物)
* High prevalence in HK due to widespread prescribing and Han Chinese genetics
* Culprit Antiepileptics:
    * **Carbamazepine** (卡馬西平)
    * **Phenytoin** (苯妥英)
    * **Lamotrigine** (拉莫三嗪)
* Culprit Antibiotics:
    * **Penicillins** (青黴素)
    * **Trimethoprim-Sulfamethoxazole / Bactrim** (複方新諾明)
* Other Culprits:
    * **Allopurinol** (別嘌醇)
    * **NSAIDs** (非類固醇消炎止痛藥)

## 2. Pathophysiology & Genetics

### Pharmacogenetics in Han Chinese (漢人藥物遺傳學)
* **HLA-B\*1502**: Strongly predicts **Carbamazepine**-induced SJS/TEN in Han Chinese (漢人)
* **HLA-B\*5801**: Strongly predicts **Allopurinol**-induced SJS/TEN in Han Chinese (漢人)
* HA Protocol: Mandatory genetic screening before prescribing specific culprits

### SJS/TEN Cellular Pathophysiology (SJS/TEN 細胞病理生理學)
* Type IV hypersensitivity reaction
* CD8+ Cytotoxic T-cells (細胞毒性T細胞) activated by drug-HLA complex
* Release of apoptotic mediators:
    * **Granulysin** (顆粒溶素) → Key marker of severity
    * **Perforin/Granzyme** (穿孔素/顆粒酶)
    * **Fas-FasL** interaction
* Result: Massive keratinocyte apoptosis → Epidermal sloughing (full-thickness burn equivalent)

### DRESS Cellular Pathophysiology (DRESS 細胞病理生理學)
* Complex interplay of immune activation + viral reactivation
* Drug-specific T-cell activation
* Reactivation of latent herpesviruses:
    * **HHV-6** (人類疱疹病毒第六型)
    * **EBV** (愛潑斯坦-巴爾病毒)
    * **CMV** (巨細胞病毒)
* Massive eosinophilic infiltration into internal organs → Tissue necrosis

## 3. Clinical Presentation

### SJS/TEN Timeline and Prodrome (SJS/TEN 時間線及前驅症狀)
* Onset: 1-3 weeks post-drug exposure
* Prodrome (1-3 days prior to rash):
    * High Fever (發燒)
    * Myalgia (肌肉痛)
    * Malaise
    * Odynophagia (吞嚥痛)

### DRESS Timeline and Prodrome (DRESS 時間線及前驅症狀)
* Onset: 2-8 weeks post-drug exposure (Delayed onset!)
* Prodrome:
    * High-spiking fever
    * Itchiness (pruritus)

## 4. Key Symptoms & Signs

### SJS/TEN Dermatological Signs (SJS/TEN 皮膚徵狀)
* **Atypical Target Lesions** (非典型靶形紅斑) → 2 colour zones (unlike classic Erythema Multiforme)
* Flaccid blisters
* Epidermal detachment (sheet-like peeling)
* **+ve Nikolsky Sign** (尼科爾斯基徵陽性) → Light lateral pressure causes epidermal shearing

### SJS/TEN Mucosal Signs (SJS/TEN 黏膜徵狀)
* ≥2 mucosal surfaces always involved
* Oral/Lips: Haemorrhagic crusting (出血性結痂)
* Ocular: Purulent conjunctivitis (結膜炎) → high risk of corneal ulceration/blindness
* Genital: Erosions
* Respiratory: Tracheal sloughing

### DRESS Cutaneous and Systemic Signs (DRESS 皮膚及全身徵狀)
* **Hallmark 1**: Massive **Facial Edema** (面部水腫)
* **Hallmark 2**: Diffuse **Morbilliform Rash** (麻疹樣皮疹)
* **Hallmark 3**: Tender generalized **Lymphadenopathy** (淋巴結腫大)
* Visceral involvement signs:
    * **Hepatomegaly** (肝腫大)
    * Jaundice (黃疸)
    * Right upper quadrant tenderness

## 5. Discriminatory Features (How to distinguish from similar conditions)

### Distinguishing SJS from Erythema Multiforme (區分 SJS 與多形性紅斑)
* Erythema Multiforme (EM) target lesions: 3 distinct colour zones
* SJS atypical target lesions: 2 distinct colour zones
* EM mucosal involvement: Rare or mild
* SJS mucosal involvement: Severe and universal

### Distinguishing DRESS from Simple Maculopapular Exanthem (區分 DRESS 與單純斑丘疹)
* Simple Exanthem:
    * No facial edema
    * No mucosal involvement
    * Normal liver/kidney function
    * Normal CBC
* DRESS:
    * Prominent facial edema
    * Internal organ failure
    * Massive eosinophilia

## 6. Differential Diagnosis

### Major Differentials for Desquamating/Systemic Rashes (脫屑性/全身性紅疹的主要鑑別診斷)

| Condition | Key Differentiating Features | Mucosal Involvement | Target Lesions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **SJS/TEN** | +ve Nikolsky, drug trigger, deep necrosis | Severe | Atypical (2 zones) |
| **SSSS** (葡萄球菌燙傷樣皮膚綜合症) | Toxin-mediated, superficial split, children <5y | Spared | Absent |
| **Kawasaki Disease** (川崎病) | Strawberry tongue, extremity desquamation later | Present | Absent |
| **Erythema Multiforme** (多形性紅斑) | HSV trigger, acral distribution | Mild/Absent | Classic (3 zones) |
| **Viral Exanthem** | Resolves quickly, normal bloods | Rare | Absent |

## 7. Tiered Investigations

### Bedside & Clinical Scoring Tools (床邊檢查及臨床評分工具)
* Clinical diagnosis based on morphology and drug history
* Calculate **SCORTEN** (TEN Severity Score) in 1st 24hrs & Day 3
    * Age >40
    * Heart Rate >120
    * Malignancy
    * Initial BSA detached >10%
    * Serum Urea >10 mmol/L
    * Serum Glucose >14 mmol/L
    * Serum Bicarbonate <20 mmol/L

### Blood Investigations for Organ Failure (血液檢查評估器官衰竭)
* **CBC**:
    * SJS/TEN: Neutropenia (poor prognostic factor), anaemia
    * DRESS: Massive **Eosinophilia** (>1.5 x 10^9/L), Atypical lymphocytes
* **Renal/Bone Panel**:
    * ↑ Creatinine (ARF)
    * Electrolyte derangement (fluid loss)
* **Liver Function Tests (LFTs)**:
    * ↑↑ ALT/AST (Fulminant hepatitis in DRESS)
    * Hyperbilirubinaemia

### Pharmacogenetic Screening Protocols (藥物遺傳學篩查方案)
* Check prior screening records in HA CMS (臨床醫療管理系統)
* Verify HLA-B\*1502 status

### Dermatological Special Investigations (皮膚科特殊檢查)
* **Skin Biopsy** (皮膚活檢):
    * SJS/TEN: Full-thickness epidermal necrosis (全層表皮壞死)
    * DRESS: Perivascular lymphocytic infiltrate, eosinophils
* Viral Serology (DRESS):
    * HHV-6 PCR
    * EBV / CMV IgM

## 8. Management (Acute, Chronic, HA Protocols)

### Immediate Universal Action (即時通用措施)
* **CRITICAL STEP**: Immediately and permanently STOP the suspected offending drug
* Insert 2x large-bore IV cannulas
* Strict isolation (Reverse barrier nursing)

### SJS/TEN Acute PICU & Burn Unit Protocol (SJS/TEN PICU 及燒傷科急性期方案)
* Admit to **PICU** or specialized **Burn Unit** (燒傷中心)
* Fluid Resuscitation:
    * Calculate using Parkland-style formulas adjusted for SJS (e.g., 2ml/kg/%BSA)
    * Target urine output: 1-2 ml/kg/hr
* Temperature Control: Avoid hypothermia (Bair Hugger)
* Infection Control:
    * Strict aseptic handling
    * Non-adherent dressings (e.g., Mepitel)
    * NO prophylactic antibiotics (promotes resistance)
* Pain Control: IV Opiate infusions (e.g., Morphine)
* **Early Ophthalmology Consult**:
    * Daily slit-lamp exam
    * Amniotic membrane grafting if needed (prevents synechiae)

### SJS/TEN Immunomodulatory Therapy (SJS/TEN 免疫調節治療)
* Administered strictly under MDT consensus in HA:
    * High-dose **IVIG** (靜脈注射免疫球蛋白)
    * **Cyclosporine** (環孢素)

### DRESS Acute Medical Protocol (DRESS 急性期內科方案)
* Admit for organ monitoring (liver/renal)
* Systemic Corticosteroids:
    * IV Methylprednisolone or Oral Prednisolone (1mg/kg)
    * **VERY slow taper** over weeks/months to prevent deadly rebounds from HHV-6 reactivation

## 9. MDT & Psychosocial Care (MSW, MCHC, SWD)

### Multidisciplinary Team Interventions (跨專科團隊介入)
* **Ophthalmology**: Prevent blindness / symblepharon
* **Dermatology / Plastic Surgery**: Skin debridement / grafting
* **Gynaecology / Urology**: Prevent genital strictures
* **Dietitian**: NGT feeding (high protein/calorie) due to oral mucosal loss

### Psychosocial Support and Alert Systems (心理社會支援及警報系統)
* Medical Social Worker (MSW) support for prolonged hospitalization
* **Mandatory CMS Allergy Alert**: Input specific drug allergy into HA computer system immediately
* Issue physical Medical Alert Bracelet (醫療警告手帶)
* Psychological support for devastating cosmetic scarring

## 10. Safeguarding & NAI Red Flags

### Non-Accidental Injury Considerations in Blistering Conditions (水泡病症中非意外傷害的考量)
* Must differentiate SJS/TEN from intentional scalding/burns
* Features against NAI:
    * Mucosal involvement
    * Target lesions
    * Recent new medication history
    * Fever prodrome

## 11. Complications & Prognosis

### SJS/TEN Acute and Chronic Complications (SJS/TEN 急性及慢性併發症)
* **Acute**:
    * Hypovolaemic shock (低血容量性休克)
    * Secondary bacterial sepsis (loss of skin barrier)
    * Mortality: up to 30% in TEN
* **Chronic**:
    * **Symblepharon** (瞼球粘連) → Permanent blindness
    * Esophageal strictures
    * Vaginal/Urethral strictures
    * Nail dystrophy / Alopecia
    * Post-inflammatory hyper/hypopigmentation

### DRESS Organ-Specific Complications (DRESS 器官特異性併發症)
* **Acute**:
    * Fulminant hepatic failure (暴發性肝衰竭) → High mortality
    * Interstitial nephritis
    * Myocarditis
* **Chronic**:
    * Autoimmune sequelae months later
    * Autoimmune Thyroiditis (Hashimoto's)
    * Type 1 Diabetes Mellitus

## 12. Examiner's Pearls (High-yield viva questions and common pitfalls)

* Trap: Using silver sulfadiazine cream routinely on the skin slough of SJS/TEN. Pearl: Sulfadiazine is a sulfa-drug, which itself is a massive trigger for SJS/TEN. Avoid all sulfa-containing topical agents.
* Viva Question: A child with epilepsy develops SJS 2 weeks after starting a new medication. The skin peels off, but you also notice their eyes are terribly red and sticking together. Why MUST an ophthalmologist be heavily involved? Answer: SJS causes severe mucosal inflammation. If the palpebral and bulbar conjunctiva are inflamed, they will scar and fuse together (symblepharon), permanently destroying the tear ducts and causing lifelong blindness.
* Pitfall: Rapidly tapering steroids in DRESS. DRESS involves viral reactivation (e.g., HHV-6); rapid steroid withdrawal leads to severe, potentially fatal flare-ups. Taper must occur extremely slowly over weeks to months.
* Viva Question: What genetic test must be ordered in Hong Kong before starting a Han Chinese patient on Carbamazepine? Answer: HLA-B*1502 screening to prevent SJS/TEN.
* Trap: Assuming a blistering child with spared mucous membranes has SJS. Pearl: Staphylococcal Scalded Skin Syndrome (SSSS) causes superficial blistering but strictly SPARES the mucous membranes, a key differentiator from SJS/TEN.
